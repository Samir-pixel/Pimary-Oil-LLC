# PRIMARY OIL L.L.C. redesign brief for Stitch AI

## Direction
Create a premium, aggressive, automotive-inspired B2B website for PRIMARY OIL L.L.C. The visual language should borrow the discipline of Ferrari.com: high contrast, confident whitespace, sharp geometry, cinematic dark sections, large product-first hero moments, and restrained motion.

Do not copy Ferrari branding and do not make racing red the main brand color. PRIMARY OIL's logo defines the palette: deep petroleum navy, black graphite, oil gold, steel gray, and white. Ferrari red may be used only as a rare alert or micro-accent, not as the dominant CTA color.

## Palette
- Petroleum navy: `#031724`
- Graphite black: `#07090B`
- Dark navy: `#061018`
- Oil gold: `#D99A12`
- Warm gold highlight: `#F2BE3E`
- Steel gray: `#66727C`
- Off white: `#F8F8F6`
- Pure white: `#FFFFFF`
- Optional velocity red: `#E8002D`

## Typography
- Headings: Oswald, uppercase, strong weight, no letter spacing.
- Body: Inter, clean and compact.
- Headlines should feel engineered and decisive, not decorative.

## UI Rules
- Use sharp or near-sharp corners: 2px buttons, 6px cards, 8px large containers.
- Use thin borders, black/gold contrast, and linear accent rules.
- Avoid soft SaaS pills, round cards, pastel gradients, and heavy blue/gold industrial calm.
- CTAs should use oil gold on dark backgrounds and invert cleanly on hover.
- Cards should feel like precision panels: flat surfaces, thin borders, restrained shadows.

## Layout
- Hero: dark cinematic background, centered bold headline, gold eyebrow, single primary CTA.
- Navigation: black translucent bar, compact uppercase links, gold hover state.
- Product cards: white panels with a thin gold top rule.
- Forms: sharp fields, gold focus state, dark CTA section.
- Mobile: keep the same contrast and sharpness; avoid oversized text that wraps poorly.
